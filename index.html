About Sign

Sign (sign.global) is a Web3 sovereign trust infrastructure for global nations. Four product pillars: EthSign (on-chain signing, 2M+ users, 1.5M contracts, future human-AI signing protocol), TokenTable (token distribution, "the Goldman Sachs of crypto," $15M revenue), Sign Pass (global identity — Sierra Leone PR, UAE Golden Visa), and Sign Protocol as the underlying layer.

One of the most anticipated Pre-TGE infrastructure projects — with Mango Labs' full-stack marketing support, Sign successfully listed on both Binance and Upbit spot markets.

Building the Narrative: How "SIGN MAKES TRUST PROGRAMMABLE" Was Born

The best narratives are simple, big, and carry philosophical weight — turning valuation models from P/E to P/Dream. Here's how we got there.

Step 1 — Product Deep Dive

We deconstructed all four product lines: EthSign (2M users, 1.5M contracts), TokenTable ($15M revenue), Sign Pass (global identity), Sign Protocol. Understood the full business landscape before touching the narrative.

Step 2 — Keyword Subtraction

Candidates: contract, trust, signature, collaboration, distribution, fairness... Following the principle of "less is more" — just like Notion only picked "tools" from dozens of possible keywords — we locked Trust as the single core keyword.

Step 3 — Philosophical Elevation

"Trust is the invisible currency that powers every transaction, every relationship, and every society." — Inspired by Yuval Noah Harari

Harari's framework gave us the civilizational dimension. Big enough, credible enough, and perfectly aligned with what Sign actually does — transactions, relationships, society.

Step 4 — Business Alignment

"In a tokenized world, blockchain provides the trust foundation for human society, transforming trust from an invisible currency into a programmable framework, and Sign sets the standard for this new era of seamless collaboration across humans, nations, and AI."

Step 5 — The Slogan

SIGN MAKES TRUST PROGRAMMABLE.

Echoes the grand theme. Describes exactly what Sign does.

Step 6 — Founder IP Amplification

Mango Labs founder Dov published "Crypto Marketing 101 Lecture 1 — How to Write a Great Narrative" as a public methodology article, using Sign as the core case study. Dual purpose: high-quality endorsement for Sign + demonstration of Mango Labs' expertise.
